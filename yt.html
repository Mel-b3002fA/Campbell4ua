We are a youtube channel, based in Germany, with the goal of strenghtening the representation of ukrainian biology content online. By crafting bespoke concepts, we create connections between ukrainian identity and science.

Combining our strive to convey knowledge across the borders of political context, strategy, art direction and production, we deliver you the content of "Campbell Biology", famously known as the Bible of Biology, in Ukrainian - short, precise and in a language we carry in our hearts.
